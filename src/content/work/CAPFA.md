### Project Overview

Our goals were to enhance speed, optimize SEO, modernize the UX/UI, and introduce a component-driven structure for higher software quality and maintainability.

#### Objectives

- **Performance and Accessibility** — Raise Google Lighthouse scores.
- **Usability and Engagement** — Redesign UX/UI to reflect CAPFA's brand.
- **Ease of Updates** — Enable CAPFA to update the site regularly via WordPress and DIVI.
- **Digital Presence** — Increase engagement through social media integration and a user-focused design.

#### Project Workflow

- **Discovery and Planning** — Established CAPFA's brand identity with a custom color palette, typography, and wireframes to boost user engagement.
- **Page Structuring** — Developed templates for pages like "Contact," "About Us," and "Projects."
- **SEO and Accessibility** — Utilized DIVI's native tools for optimized SEO and accessibility.
- **Component-Driven Structure** — Incorporated custom PHP functions and DIVI's modular tools for streamlined backend management.
- **Dynamic Features** — Implemented JavaScript for an interactive WhatsApp button without performance-impacting plugins.
- **Testing and Optimization** — Achieved a 94% Google Lighthouse performance score with further validation through Google Analytics and additional testing tools.
- **Execution Timeline** — Followed a structured weekly timeline to ensure project completion on schedule.

#### Results and Impact

- **Performance** — Boosted Lighthouse score from 45% to 94%, delivering faster load times and enhanced user retention.
- **Usability and Engagement** — Improved user interaction and accessibility, enabling seamless connections between CAPFA's social media and website.
- **Client Empowerment** — CAPFA now manages content updates independently, solidifying its digital presence and engagement strategy.
